Motorcycles
Vehicles below 3.5 t

Standard speed limits (unless otherwise stated by traffic signs) [km/h]

Urban roads

Non-urban roads with no/narrow hard shoulder (<1.5 m) 90 km/h for motorcycles; 80 km/h if carrying dangerous goods 45 km/h for mopeds, tricycles & light quads

Non-urban roads with hard shoulder (>1.5 m) or dual carriageway 100 km/h for motorcycles. 90 km/h if carrying dangerous goods 45 km/h for mopeds, tricycles & light quads

Motorways/expressways 120 km/h for motorcycles. 110km/h if carrying dangerous goods Mopeds, tricycles and light quads are not allowed on motorways/expressways

Passenger cars, vans
Vehicles below 3.5 t

Standard speed limits (unless otherwise stated by traffic signs) [km/h]

Urban roads 40 km/h if carrying dangerous goods

Non-urban roads with no/narrow hard shoulder (<1.5 m) 90 km/h for passenger cars 80 km/h for light vans (passenger car derivative vans) 70 km/h for vans and light trucks 70 km/h for passenger cars & vans towing a trailer

Non-urban roads with hard shoulder at least 1.5 m wide or with two lanes or more in each direction 100 km/h for passenger cars. 90 km/h light vans (passenger cars derivative vans) 80 km/h for vans and light trucks 80 km/h for passenger cars or vans towing a trailer 10 km/h slower than the normal speed limit if carrying dangerous goods

Motorways/expressways 120 km/h for passenger cars 100 km/h for light vans (passenger car derivative vans) 90 km/h vans and light trucks 90 km /h for passenger cars or vans towing a light trailer of less than 750 kg (Type O1.) 10 km/h slower than the normal speed limit if carrying dangerous goods

Passenger cars & vans with trailers
Vehicles below 3.5 t

Standard speed limits (unless otherwise stated by traffic signs) [km/h]

Urban roads 40 km/h if carrying dangerous goods

Non-urban roads with no/narrow hard shoulder (<1.5 m) 10 km/h slower than the normal speed limit if carrying dangerous goods

Non-urban roads with hard shoulder (>1.5 m) or dual carriageway 10 km/h slower than the normal speed limit if carrying dangerous goods

Motorways/expressways 10 km/h slower than the normal speed limit if carrying dangerous goods

Non-urban roads with hard shoulder (>1.5 m) or dual carriageway 10 km/h slower than the normal speed limit if carrying dangerous goods

Buses

Standard speed limits (unless otherwise stated by traffic signs) [km/h]

Urban roads

Non-urban roads with no/narrow hard shoulder (<1.5 m) 80 km/h for buses allowed to carry standing passengers outside urban areas 80 km/h for school buses

Non-urban roads with hard shoulder (>1.5 m) or dual carriageway 80 km/h for buses allowed to carry standing passengers outside urban areas 80 km/h for school buses

Motorways/expressways 80 km/h for buses allowed to carry standing passengers outside urban areas 90 km/h for school buses
