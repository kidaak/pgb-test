Use of mobile phone without hand-free set

Use of mobile phone with hand-free set

  • It is not allowed to drive a motor vehicle whilst wearing or using on both ears earphones or headphones or any other device which is likely to hinder the driver from hearing properly
  • It is not allowed to drive a motor vehicle whilst holding or using a hand-held mobile telephone or any other similar hand-held device, other than a hand-free device or a two-way radio
