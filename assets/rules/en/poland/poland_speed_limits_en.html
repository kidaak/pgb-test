Motorcycles
(Vehicles below 3.5 t)

Standard speed limits (unless otherwise stated by traffic signs) [km/h]

Urban roads 50 km/h (5 a.m. – 11 p.m.) 60 km/h (11 p.m. – 5 a.m.)

Non-urban roads 100 km/h on dual carriageway Limits for quadricycles the same as for vehicles over 3.5 t; 45 km/h for light quadricycles

Motorways/expressways Expressway: 120 km/h Motorway: 140 km/h; 100 km/h on single carriageway (expressway). No entry for quadricycles
