Daytime running lights are mandatory even during daylight hours Mandatory for motorcycles and mopeds Mandatory for the other vehicles only during winter time (daylight saving time)

Mandatory use of winter tyres Winter tyres are mandatory in wintry conditions. (cars weighing less than 3.5t may use snow chains on powered wheels) Busses and heavy goods vehicles must either carry snow chains or have winter tyres (M+S) installed on powered wheels. Studded tyres are not allowed.

Safety equipment for cyclists Reflective vest (outside built-up areas and at night/in poor visibility)
